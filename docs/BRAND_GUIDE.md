## üìê Logo Analysis & Usage

### 1. The Symbol (Gradient Hex/Brackets)
*   **Meaning:** Represents the convergence of **Code** (`< >`) and **Execution** (Checkmark).
*   **Vibe:** Dynamic, Full-Stack, Fluid.
*   **Placement:** Use as the favicon and key visual element.

### 2. The Wordmark (DIGIDEV AGENCY)
*   **Font:** Geometric Sans (Custom/modified).
*   **Color:** White (on Dark) or Dark Navy (on Light).
*   **Style:** Modern, Tracking (Letter spacing) loose.

## üí° UX/UI Principles

1.  **"Glow, Don't Shout":** Use colored drop shadows (Glows) instead of solid blocks of color.
2.  **Glass is Class:** Use backdrop-blur heavily to create depth over the black background.
3.  **Motion:** The gradient should subtly animate (hue-rotate) on hover.
